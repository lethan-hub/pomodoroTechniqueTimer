A pomodoro technique timer is a time-managment method used to improve focus and productivity by breaking work into 25 minute sessions followed by 5 minute breaks. With this method, individuals avoid burnouts, boost concentration, and avoids procrascination.

Steps:
1. Choose a task wanting to complete.
2. Click the "Start" button and do the task for 25 minutes.
3. Work on the task until the timer rings.
4. Take a short break (5 minutes).
5. Repeat this process four times.
6. Take a long break (15 minutes) after four pomodoros.
7. Repeat until task is completed or until you need.
